About Me!

About Me I'm a designer and programmer working as a 3D Generalist. I hold a BA in Film & Animation with minors in Computer Science and Digital Marketing. For as long as I can remember, I've been making things. During my journey as a "maker", I've taken the idea of being a "generalist" to heart, combining programming techniques and real-world problem solving to create totally unique visuals. Today, I've worked with large IPs, companies, and people to create stunning brand worlds and experiences. A little bit of wiring circuits here, a bit of building dioramas there, and a whole lot of 3D work. From 3D printing physical dioramas and adding visual effects to them, to compositing effects into live action footage, to programmatically controlling camera settings based on lighting, I take pride in being able to connect with technology and use it in novel ways. Along the way, I've become a B2B marketer, and someone who engages with brands in a professional, methodical manner. - CR
